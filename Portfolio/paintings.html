BFA Thesis Show, Metamorphosis

Artist Statement:

CLOSED CAPTIONS FOR IMAGE ABOVE:

I am a nonbinary artist working with illustration and a graphic style in my acrylic paintings. In my work, I portray the joys and struggles of transitioning using self portraiture, thorns, moths, and bats. The lunar moth is a metaphorical motif in my work that not only represents my transition itself, but also my identity. Bats, a natural predator of the lunar moth, symbolize family members and familial trauma. In my work, I hope to reflect that pain, joy, and comfort can all be a part of transitioning.
